About The Bonhamizer

This is a music hack created at Music Hack Day San Francisco 2013 by Paul Lamere. It is powered by The Echo Nest. This hack was created using the same technology as the Infinite Jukebox, Infinite Gangnam Style and Bohemian Rhapsichord.

This app will take a track and re-render it as if John Bonham of Led Zepplin was the drummer. This app works by cutting songs up into beats and tatums, and aligning the beats to John Bonham Drum patterns. The drum patterns are outtakes from the "In through the Out Door" and sourced from SaladRecords.com

Special thanks to Matthew Santiago for drumming consulation.
